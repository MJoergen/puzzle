# puzzle
Place thirteen pentominos on in an 8x8 box.

The program finds all 129168 solutions in about 20 minutes (including writing all solutions to a file).

One such solution is:

12 12 12 10 10 10 10  3 
12 12 11  4  4 10  3  3 
 6 11 11 11  4  3  3  9 
 6  6  6 11  4  4  9  9 
 6  2  2  2  2  2  9  8 
 5  5  5  7  7  0  9  8 
 5  1  1  7  0  0  0  8 
 5  1  1  7  7  0  8  8
